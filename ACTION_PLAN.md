## 🚀 STEP-BY-STEP ACTION PLAN: HOW TO START SELLING

### PHASE 1: CHOOSE YOUR POSITION (Week 1-2)

You have 3 main options. Pick ONE to start:

#### Option A: AFFILIATE CREATOR (Lowest Risk, Lowest Capital)
**What:** Promote other brands' products and earn commission
**Capital needed:** IDR 0 - 500K (just phone + internet)
**Monthly potential:** IDR 500K - 15M+

| Step | Action | Timeline |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Create TikTok account (niche: Muslim fashion) | Day 1 |
| 2 | Register as TikTok Shop Creator: TikTok App → Profile → TikTok Shop Creator Center | Day 1 |
| 3 | Browse Muslim fashion products (mukena, koko, pashmina) in Creator Center | Day 1 |
| 4 | Apply to promote products from Tiebymin, Tazbiya, Khadijah Label, Rabbani | Day 1-2 |
| 5 | Request free samples (many brands send samples to new affiliates) | Day 2-3 |
| 6 | Create first 3 videos: unboxing, try-on, "honest review" | Day 3-5 |
| 7 | Register on Shopee App → Share products with affiliate links | Day 3-5 |
| 8 | Post 1-2 videos daily for 30 days | Ongoing |
| 9 | Apply to agencies: Gushcloud, SociaBuzz, GetCraft | Week 2 |
| 10 | After 10K followers: start doing TikTok Live selling | Month 2-3 |

**Stores to start with (confirmed affiliate programs):**
- Tiebymin (pashmina, IDR 55K-75K, 8-12% commission)
- Tazbiya (mukena, IDR 100K-350K, 8-12% commission)
- Khadijah Label (premium mukena, IDR 150K-500K, 10-15% commission)
- Rabbani (koko + mukena, IDR 120K-400K, 8-12% commission)
- Alluna Official (mukena travel, IDR 100K-300K, 8-12% commission)

#### Option B: RESELLER / DROPSHIPPER (Low Capital, Medium Risk)
**What:** Buy wholesale from Tasikmalaya producers, sell on Shopee/TikTok
**Capital needed:** IDR 1-5 Million
**Monthly potential:** IDR 3M - 30M+

| Step | Action | Timeline |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Research products: search "mukena tasikmalaya" / "koko tasikmalaya" on Shopee | Day 1 |
| 2 | Contact 3-5 Tasikmalaya producers via Shopee chat or WhatsApp | Day 1-3 |
| 3 | Order samples (5-10 pcs each) to check quality | Day 3-7 |
| 4 | Create Shopee Seller account: seller.shopee.co.id | Day 3 |
| 5 | Create TikTok Shop Seller account: seller-id.tiktok.com | Day 3 |
| 6 | List products with optimized titles, photos, descriptions | Day 7-10 |
| 7 | Enable Shopee AMS (Affiliate Marketing Solution) — set 10% commission | Day 10 |
| 8 | Enable TikTok Shop Open Plan affiliate | Day 10 |
| 9 | Start daily Shopee Live (1-2 hours) | Day 10-14 |
| 10 | Send samples to 20 micro-influencers for affiliate content | Week 2-3 |
| 11 | Scale: order more stock, hire livestream host, expand product line | Month 2+ |

**Where to source products:**
- Tasikmalaya konveksi (search Shopee for direct producers)
- Tanah Abang Jakarta (fabric + ready-made)
- Bandung garment factories

#### Option C: BRAND OWNER (Highest Potential, Highest Investment)
**What:** Create your own brand, design products, manufacture in Tasikmalaya
**Capital needed:** IDR 10-50 Million
**Monthly potential:** IDR 10M - 500M+

| Step | Action | Timeline |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Choose your niche: mukena OR koko OR pashmina (pick ONE first) | Week 1 |
| 2 | Study competitors: buy top 5 products on Shopee; analyze quality, pricing, packaging | Week 1 |
| 3 | Design your product (or adapt trending designs with unique bordir/patterns) | Week 1-2 |
| 4 | Visit/contact Tasikmalaya konveksi for OEM production | Week 2-3 |
| 5 | Order first batch: 100-200 pcs MOQ | Week 3-4 |
| 6 | Register brand (DGIP trademark) | Week 3-4 |
| 7 | Create branded packaging (logo, hang tag, care label, thank you card) | Week 3-4 |
| 8 | Set up Shopee Official Store + TikTok Shop | Week 4 |
| 9 | Product photography: professional shots + lifestyle shots | Week 4-5 |
| 10 | Launch with 20-30 affiliate creators (send free samples) | Week 5-6 |
| 11 | Daily Shopee Live + TikTok Live | Week 6+ |
| 12 | Participate in mega sales: 9.9, 11.11, 12.12, Ramadan campaign | Ongoing |

### PHASE 3: CONTENT STRATEGY (Ongoing)

**Daily Content Calendar:**

| Day | Content Type | Platform |
|-----|-------------|----------|
| Monday | Product showcase video (15-60 sec) | TikTok + Shopee Video |
| Tuesday | Try-on haul / styling tips | TikTok |
| Wednesday | Shopee Live (1-2 hours) | Shopee Live |
| Thursday | "Honest review" / comparison video | TikTok |
| Friday | TikTok Live (1-2 hours) | TikTok Live |
| Saturday | Behind-the-scenes / production video | TikTok |
| Sunday | Customer testimonial / UGC repost | TikTok + Shopee Video |

**Content Tips:**
- Always show the product on a person (not just flat lay)
- Use trending TikTok sounds/music
- Include price in video (Indonesian viewers want to know)
- Use hashtags: #fashionmuslim #mukena #kokopria #pashmina #shopeehaul #tiktokmademebuyit
- Post between 6PM-10PM WIB (peak engagement)

### PHASE 4: AFFILIATE NETWORK BUILDING (Month 2+)

**Target: 50-200 active affiliates promoting your products**

| Week | Action |
|------|--------|
| Week 1 | Enable AMS on Shopee (set 10-15% commission) + Open Plan on TikTok |
| Week 2 | Send DMs to 30 micro-influencers (1K-10K followers) with free sample offer |
| Week 3 | Join Facebook groups: "Shopee Affiliate Indonesia", "TikTok Affiliate Indonesia" |
| Week 4 | Post in groups: "OPEN AFFILIATE — Free samples + 10-15% commission" |
| Week 5+ | Register on Gushcloud, SociaBuzz, GetCraft as a brand seeking creators |
| Ongoing | Track top-performing affiliates → offer higher commission (15-20%) → exclusive deals |

**DM Script (ready to use):**
```
Halo kak! 🙏 Saya dari [Brand Name]. Kami lihat konten kakak bagus banget! 
Mau tawarin kerjasama affiliate untuk produk [mukena/koko/pashmina] kami. 
Komisi 10-15% + free sample produk. Berminat? 😊
```

### PHASE 5: SCALE (Month 3-6)

| Goal | Action |
|------|--------|
| Increase product range | Add 2-3 new products per month |
| Hire livestream host | IDR 3-8M/month; daily 2-4 hour shifts |
| Expand to Tokopedia | List same products; enable Tokopedia Live |
| Build D2C website | Shopify/WooCommerce; WhatsApp for orders |
