Research compilation of top products from **Sinar Toko Tiga** and **Toko Tiga** sewing machine stores across Shopee and TikTok Shop.

## Key Findings

- **Sinar Toko Tiga**: 55K followers, 898 products, rating 4.87 on Shopee
- **TOKO TIGA**: 78K followers, 1,748 products, rating 4.88 on Shopee  
- **TikTok**: 25.6K followers, 641 videos, 199.5K likes
- **Best Sellers**: Singer Simple 3232, Singer 4432 Heavy Duty, Singer 4423
- **Price Range**: Rp 7,447 (oil) to Rp 26,000,000 (Janome MC 500e)
- **Main Brands**: Singer, Janome, Brother, Butterfly, Typical, Toyota, Riccar
